The Power of Fire - 2D Platformer Game
2D parkour platformer focused on dynamic movement mechanics

I designed and developed a fast-paced 2D platformer that emphasizes fluid movement mechanics, focusing on rocket jumps and dash abilities. The game features dynamic level design that gradually introduces complexity while maintaining engaging gameplay. Working closely with a team of playtesters, I iterated on the game's feel and responsiveness based on regular user feedback, resulting in precisely tuned movement physics that feels both challenging and satisfying.

NLP Conversation Entailment Project
Research project on conversational AI and natural language understanding

This research project addressed the challenge of improving conversational entailment models for multi-turn dialogue systems. I developed a hierarchical attention-based neural network architecture that more effectively captures the context and semantic relationships in complex conversations. The model was designed to understand implications and unstated assumptions in natural dialogue, significantly improving coherence in AI conversation systems.

A major component of this work involved fine-tuning state-of-the-art large language models including Mistral 7B and LLaMA. I implemented custom training pipelines and evaluation methodologies to optimize these models for conversational context awareness. The research culminated in co-authoring a paper that proposes novel approaches to multi-turn dialogue understanding, with our findings indicating a substantial improvement over baseline methods in maintaining contextual coherence across multiple conversation turns.
